Recommended Reading

These books have shaped my view of how programming should be. This is not an exhaustive list, but rather a list of what I find most valuable and recommend most often.

Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs

Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs, by Abelson, Sussman, and Sussman, is one of the single greatest resources available in the field of computer science.

This book is available for free online here.

The Tao of Programming

Programmers, myself included, have a tendency to overengineer their projects. Of the books on this list I re-read The Tao of Programming, by Geoffry James, most frequently.

This book is available for free online here.
